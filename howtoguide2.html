How to Survive a Dinosaur Attack

A treacherous jungle guide for daring kids

Quick Steps to Stay Alive

  • Hold your breath in the shadows
    The jungle grows silent as colossal footsteps thunder closer. One wrong gasp could betray you.
  • Listen for the bone-chilling roar
    When the air trembles, you know the predator hunts nearby. Stay low, stay hidden.
  • Seek refuge among twisted roots
    The gnarled jungle floor may be your only shield against razor teeth and piercing claws.
  • Freeze when the beast’s eyes glow
    Movement invites doom. Become a statue, a shadow, a whisper in the foliage.
  • Distract with sacrifice
    Toss away your pack or trinket. Let the monster’s hunger chase the decoy, not you.
  • Escape through treacherous terrain
    Lead the beast into thorny thickets and rocky climbs where its bulk betrays it.
  • Signal your allies in silence
    A hand gesture in the dark may save your team. Speak not, lest the predator hear.
  • Know your enemy
    A towering tyrant stalks differently than a cunning raptor. Study their terror, plan your survival.
  • Celebrate survival at dawn
    If you endure the night, rejoice. The jungle remembers those who outwit its monsters.
